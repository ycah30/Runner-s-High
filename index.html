RUNNING TIPS

PROPER BREATHING
TECHNIQUE

To enhance running efficiency and reduce fatigue, practice deep, rhythmic breathing. Inhale over 2-3 steps, hold for 1-2 steps, and exhale over 2-3 steps.

DYNAMIC
WARM-UPS

Replace static stretches with dynamic warm-ups such as leg swings, high knees, and lunges. These exercises improve flexibility and help lower the risk of injury.

CADENCE AND
STRIDE

Focus on maintaining a quick turnover and a midfoot or forefoot strike. This approach reduces impact and improves overall running efficiency.

HYDRATION AND
ELECTROLYTES

Ensure proper hydration by drinking water or a sports drink about 30 minutes before your run. During longer runs, consume electrolyte-rich foods or supplements to prevent dehydration and cramping.

GRADUAL
PROGRESSION

Avoid abrupt changes to your running routine to reduce the risk of injury and burnout. Gradually increase your mileage, intensity, and frequency to allow your body to adapt effectively.

ABOUT US

OUR STORY

Runner's High was founded in 2024 by a group of passionate runners who wanted to create a supportive community for like-minded individuals. Our mission is to provide a platform where runners of all levels can connect, share their experiences, and motivate each other to reach their goals.

OUR VALUES

At Runner's High, we foster a welcoming community where all runners, regardless of background or fitness level, feel supported and motivated. Our core values focus on inclusivity, providing support through shared advice, and inspiring members to achieve their running goals, from their first 5K to a running marathon.

OUR TEAM

Our team consists of experienced runners who are passionate about the sport and committed to fostering a positive and supportive community, including our certified personal trainer, Coach Angelo Flores.

Lace up your shoes, hit the pavement, and experience the runner's high with us!
